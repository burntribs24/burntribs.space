Welcome to the official overview of the USMS department within Florida State Roleplay. This department is invite only and operates under strict federal protocol guidelines.

Division Purpose

  • Execute federal arrest warrants and fugitive apprehension.
  • Protect federal witnesses and judicial officials.
  • Oversee high-risk extraditions and prisoner transport.
  • Support local LEO agencies with federal level backup.
  • Handle high priority manhunts and emergency warrants.

Standard Operations

  • Operate unmarked or minimally marked federal vehicles.
  • Deploy with tactical gear, ballistic vests, and breaching tools.
  • Use federal radios and secure comms (discord only).
  • Work in coordinated teams, never solo unless cleared.
  • Strict chain of command; direct orders come from Command Staff or Federal Liaison.

Requirements

  1. Must be active within the RP community and pass a background check.
  2. Invitation or recommendation required from a command level member.
  3. Demonstrate exceptional roleplay, communication, and tactical awareness.
  4. Follow all federal RP rules and vehicle/gear regulations.
  5. Maintain professionalism and confidentiality at all times.

Important Notes

This division is invite only and not accessible to the public. If you have questions or believe you're eligible, please contact @BurntRibs24, or @wrdto
